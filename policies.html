Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Kodai Blossom from Kodaikanal Lake?
Kodai Blossom is located just 2 km from Kodaikanal Lake, making it easy to access the main town, boating, and popular tourist attractions.
Is this homestay suitable for families and group stays?
Yes, our cottage homestay in Kodaikanal is ideal for families and large groups, with multiple room options and a total capacity of up to 25–35 guests.
What types of rooms are available?
We offer Deluxe Rooms, Triple Sharing Rooms, Family Units, and large cottage setups with multiple bedrooms and hall spaces, suitable for couples, families, and group stays.
Do you provide food at the homestay?
Yes, we serve fresh home cooked food from our in-house kitchen.
What amenities are available at the property?
Our Kodaikanal homestay includes WiFi, parking, hot water, a private garden, indoor / outdoor games, badminton court, campfire and barbeque setup (on request), and a travel desk for local sightseeing.
Is parking available at the property?
Yes, we provide free parking space for guests staying at Kodai Blossom.
How can I reach the property?
The homestay is located near Upper Lake Road in Kodaikanal, around 2 km from the bus stand, 90 km from Kodai Road railway station, and 120 km from Madurai airport.
